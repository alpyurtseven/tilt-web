Frequently Asked Questions

schedule When does the maze reset?

A new maze is generated every 24 hours at midnight UTC. Everyone in the world gets the exact same maze to solve that day!

warning What are the time penalties?

Each wall hit adds a penalty that grows with each collision — early bumps cost less, but repeated hits add up fast. The penalty caps at 2.10s per hit, so precision matters more the further you go.

history Can I replay old mazes?

You can view your past performance in the Archive section, but daily mazes can only be played competitively on their specific day.

sync Is my progress saved?

Yes! Your runs and times are automatically saved to your device's local storage. No account needed.
