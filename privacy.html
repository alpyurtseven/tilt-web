Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

TILT is a daily maze game for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data TILT does and does not collect.

Data We Do Not Collect

TILT does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration or login required
  • No name, email address, or contact information collected
  • No location data collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No data shared with third parties

Local Storage

Your game results and run history are saved locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

Since TILT collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages.
